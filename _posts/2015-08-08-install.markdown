## Cocoapods
Mirmeca can be installed through Cocoapods.

To do so, your `Podfile` should look something like this:

<pre class="prettyprint">
source 'https://github.com/CocoaPods/Specs.git'
platform :ios, '8.0'
use_frameworks!

pod 'Mirmeca', '~> 0.04'
</pre>
