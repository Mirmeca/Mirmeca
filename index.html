Why use Mirmeca?


Easy to use

Mirmeca lets you query for content on your WordPress backend in 3 lines of code. Mirmeca also gives you simple models to manipulate the data retrieved. The documentation lists all the attributes for each model.

Cut dev time

Mirmeca packs everything you need to be ready in minutes. From Gateways to retrieve any type of content from your WordPress backend to Models to manage this content. Be ready in minutes, not days!
